Eligibility & Admission Process

Understand your eligibility, prepare the necessary documents, and follow our streamlined 7-step roadmap to start your studies.

📋

Documents Required

Make sure to keep digital scans and original copies of these records ready for your registration.

  • ✓ 10th Marksheet
  • ✓ 12th Marksheet
  • ✓ Aadhar Card
  • ✓ NEET Score / Admit Card
  • ✓ Passport (Original copy)
  • ✓ Digital Photo (3.5x4.5cm, white BG)
🎯

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓ 12th Result Criteria Minimum 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry & Biology (PCB) aggregate for General Category, and 40% for ST/SC/OBC categories.
  • ✓ NEET Qualification Status The candidate must be NEET qualified in the current or preceding two academic years.
  • ✓ Age Requirement The applicant must be 17 years of age or older by December 31st of the year of admission.
💡

Note: Ensure all documents are valid, official, and up-to-date. Incomplete papers can delay state registration or visa processing.

🗺️

Your Journey Roadmap

Follow these structured milestones from initial sign-up to landing at your medical university.

01

Registration

Complete your initial registration form with basic details and submit scanned marksheets.

02

Admission Letter

Receive your formal admission letter from the university directly on your registered email.

03

Examination

Appear for any standard verification or university registration entrance test if required.

04

Contract Papers

Submit and verify your signed university educational service contract agreements.

05

Invitation Letter

Receive the official government invitation letter from the university (critical for visa issue).

06

Flight Booking & Visa

Submit your passport for visa processing and get your flight tickets block-booked.

07

Fly

Fly to your destination city in groups with an attendant to begin your dream medical career!
