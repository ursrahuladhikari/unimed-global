Eligibility & Admission Guide

Understand your eligibility, follow our 7-step roadmap, and prepare the right documents — all in one place.

Step Zero

Eligibility Criteria

Confirm you meet these requirements before applying.

12th Result Criteria

Minimum 50% marks in PCB aggregate for General Category, and 40% for ST/SC/OBC categories.

NEET Qualification

Must be NEET qualified in the current or preceding two academic years with a valid scorecard.

Age Requirement

Applicant must be 17 years or older by December 31st of the year of admission.

Who Can Apply
